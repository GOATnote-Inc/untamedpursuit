# Scene: First Meeting

The soldiers kept distance from the fort corral.

Eve studied the big mare. Three cavalrymen had learned about overconfidence.

She takes a saddle just fine Eve said. She just chooses who puts it on.

## The Three Mares

Queen noticed the change first. Three riders cutting them off.

Storm moved first. No hesitation. Pure power.

Three mares three riders moving like they trained together.
