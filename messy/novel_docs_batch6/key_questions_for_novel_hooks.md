# Key Questions for Novel Hooks - Fierce

## Main Timeline
1. Eve on the run from bounty hunters toward San Francisco
2. Part 1 The Chase - pursued by Silas, reunites with Colt
3. Part 2 Alliances - Eve Silas Colt form uneasy alliance
4. Part 3 Hunters Shadow - Silas betrays group captures Eve
5. Part 4 Confrontation - Eve plans to confront Samuel Taylor
6. Part 5 Showdown - Eve storms Taylors compound
7. Cliffhanger - Colt agrees to help rescue Silas

## Key Flashbacks
- Childhood: emotional depth
- Midwifery: medical expertise
- Jessie friendship: humor heartbreak
- Brothers teachings: wisdom

## Self-Compassion Themes
- Jessie encourages humor and self-kindness
- Healing and self-compassion woven throughout
- Eve gradually reflects and applies advice
- Jessie key message: love yourself
- Eve learns self-love is key to healing
