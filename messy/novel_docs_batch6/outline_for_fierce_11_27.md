# FIERCE Book One of the Untamed Pursuit Series

## ACT I THE HUNTER 25000 words

Ch1 Mountains Edge - Opens at dawn, Eve on mountain ridge with Jack. Storm concealed. Eve tracks target through rifle scope. Target is human trafficker. Eves impossible shot.

Ch2 Descent - Chaos after shot. Eve and Jack escape. Storm proves her worth. Pursuit. Claras extraction team doesnt appear.

Ch3 Messages in Blood - Safe house reveals plot. Jack gets coded message about Claras condition. Target connected to old enemies.

Ch4 Glass Walls - Arrival at Claras estate. Eve sees Clara since illness severe. Thomas Mitchell arrives.

Ch5 Old Wounds - Clara reveals condition. First Eve-Thomas interaction. Jack warns about Thomas.

Ch6 Night Blooms - Eves midnight practice interrupted by Thomas. First chemistry. Clara reveals target importance.

Ch7 Training Days - Flashback young Eve with Clara. Eve training with Thomas. First attack on estate.

Ch8 Battle Lines - Eve defending estate. Thomas helpful but suspicious. Deeper conspiracy.

Ch9 Trust Falls - Eve chooses loyalties. Clara health downturn. Attack on Glass Garden begins.

## ACT II THE CHASE 30000 words

Ch10-12 Glass Garden assault. Retreat to tunnels. Thomas reveals Durand connection. Epic canyon escape.

Ch13-18 Group splits. Eve and Thomas work together. Clara reveals plan. Eve trains recruits. Covert op. Major victory with cost.

## ACT III THE PREY 25000 words

Ch19-24 Clara reveals final secrets. Battle for Glass Garden. Eves impossible shot saves Thomas. Clara critical.

## ACT IV THE LEGACY 20000 words

Ch25-28 Claras passing. Network accepts Eve. Final scene mirrors opening with Eve as mentor.
