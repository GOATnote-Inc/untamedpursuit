# Novel Development Roadmap for Fierce

1. Manuscript Dev: expand scenes, strengthen emotions, add character layers
2. Refinement: pacing, chapter hooks, dialogue polish
3. Beta Readers: feedback on pacing and emotional impact
4. Professional Editing: developmental, copy, proofread
5. Audiobook: natural dialogue, find narrator
6. Production: format ebook print, cover design, trailer
7. Publish: KDP or traditional, Audible ACX
8. Long-Term: fan community, reviews guide sequels
