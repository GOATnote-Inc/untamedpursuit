# Shifting Light

Clara dining room caught the last rays of sunset. The formal table had been set for ten.

Clara at the head, Samuel at the foot. Jessie to Clara right, Eve to her left. Morgan and Colt opposite. Maria watching both doors.

The others will join us shortly, Samuel said. Silas is bringing our engineer.

Pete, Jessie cut in. Your explosives expert is Crazy Pete from the mining camp.

We prefer Demolitions Specialist, Clara corrected.

Eve caught Colt watching her. He had taught her long-range shooting years ago.

The door opened. Silas entered followed by Dink.

One by one, the pieces clicked. Every significant figure from their lives, carefully positioned.

This is about protection, Clara said. On a scale most people never see.

A team, Samuel added. Medical expertise. Combat ability. Intelligence gathering.

Six months, Samuel said quietly. Clara has helped create this team and would like to weigh in while she can.

I am in, Jessie said.

When do we start? Eve asked.

My dear, you started years ago.

Time to see what they could become together.
