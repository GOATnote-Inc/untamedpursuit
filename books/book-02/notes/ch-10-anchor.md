## Chapter: Book 2, Chapter 10 — "The Intimacy of Irrelevance"
## POV: Clara Chen — lowering her guard. The architectural vocabulary reaching for emotional content. The full father story, promised in Book 1 ch-06, delivered here.

## Outline Beats
1. Evening on the stern. Calm water. Stars above, phosphorescence below. Clara and Samuel sit together.
2. Clara tells Samuel about her father — the full story, not the fragment from Book 1 ch-06. Canton trading routes, three Pacific crossings, how he taught her to fight and why. "A Chen does not fail" — what it meant to a girl, what it means now.
3. Samuel tells about his mother. The house in Wexford, then Philadelphia. How she died quietly. What she smelled like. What she said on Sundays.
4. The intimacy of irrelevance — not strategy, not Wallace, not survival. The things that made them.
5. Clara leans against Samuel. Twelve inches collapsed. Trust, not desire.
