# Compaction Anchor — Book 2, Chapter 6

## Chapter
Book 2, Chapter 6 — "Departure"

## POV
Clara Chen. She has buried Harper, survived cholera, watched Davis die before they cleared the harbor. She is now aboard the Resolute, entering the Pacific. Her grief is being channeled into operational control — she is absorbing Harper's role without deciding to. Her metaphor domain is architectural/spatial: structures, sight lines, load-bearing walls, the geometry of rooms and combat.

## Outline Beats
1. The Resolute clears Panama City harbor. Clara stands at the stern rail watching the city shrink — the city where she buried Harper. The last connection to the Atlantic coast breaks as they round the headland.
2. The ship is smaller than the Caroline. Tight quarters, a new crew, Captain Morales running a practical operation. Clara assigns cabins, establishes routines, maps the ship's layout with the eye Harper trained her to use.
3. Clara assigns herself Harper's watch rotation. The pre-dawn watch, 4 to 6 AM. George beside her. She walks the deck the way Harper walked the perimeter of her townhouse — checking lines of approach, noting crew movements, listening.
4. The Pacific opens before them. Vast, flat, featureless. After the jungle's claustrophobia, the ocean's emptiness is its own kind of pressure. Clara has crossed from the Atlantic to the Pacific. Everything behind her is behind her.

## Bible Facts Loaded
- Clara: architectural/spatial metaphors, dark gallows wit, "A Chen does not fail," leather coat, Colts, sword cane, Canton medical training. Has absorbed Harper's habits without noticing (from ch-04's Samuel observation). Takes Harper's watch rotation deliberately.
- Captain Morales: brown-skinned, weathered, Spanish accent, practical, unsentimental. Two-masted coastal trader. Does not shake hands (cholera). Recognized Clara as "the one in charge."
- Samuel: merchant metaphors, calloused hands, powerful build. Has already been given the negotiation/provisioning role by Clara.
- Lily: violin, paints, steady and quiet since Panama City. Artist metaphor domain.
- George: 140 lbs, hand signals. Now responds to Clara's commands (command transfer at the grave). Silent.
- The Resolute: two-masted coastal trader, Captain Morales's ship.
- Company: Clara, Samuel, Lily, Reyes, Cole, Jennings, George, Ajax — six passengers, two dogs.
