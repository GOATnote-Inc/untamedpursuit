# Compaction Anchor — Book 2, Chapter 14

## Chapter: Book 2, Chapter 14
- **Title**: "The California Coast"
- **POV**: Samuel Taylor — processing Lily's story, approaching California. Merchant/commercial metaphor domain.
- **Timeline**: Late May -- early June 1847
- **Location**: Aboard the Resolute, Pacific Ocean (approaching California coast)

## Outline Beats
1. Samuel processes Lily's story in his private journal. "He taught her what I could not." Crosses it out. Writes: "What I would not." The distinction matters — Samuel could have taught Lily to shoot, chose not to enter that space.
2. California coast appears on the horizon — brown hills, green valleys, occasional settlement. Samuel counts: miles of coast, harbors, places Clara could establish. Merchant's inventory applied to a continent.
3. Samuel thinks about Thomas — not suspicion yet, but distance. Thomas is 3,000 miles and two coded letters away. The system Thomas built to maintain control is also the system that separates them. Samuel wonders what Thomas knows, suspects, does alone in the office with Persian rugs and tactical maps.
4. A letter to Thomas composed in Samuel's mind but never written — things about Lily's story, Harper's teaching, California coast that Thomas's codes cannot carry.
5. Land birds. The smell of sage. California.

## Bible Facts Loaded
- **Samuel**: Merchant/commercial metaphors (ledgers, cargo, balance sheets, manifests, arithmetic of value and cost). Candid, direct, comfortable with silence. Father from Wexford, Irish dock worker. Mother Mary — died when Samuel was 17 (lungs, coal dust, lye soap/bread dough/lavender). Weeps openly when moved. Boxing-trained. First combat use of Paterson Colt at mule-trail ambush. Two records: coded letters to Thomas (morning, brisk strokes), private journal (evening, deliberate strokes).
- **Thomas correspondence system**: Substitution cipher, key changes per port. Kingston key: Adam Smith. Panama City key: Blackstone's Commentaries. Red wax seal, brass case, initials T.A. interlocked. Three-port routing: Kingston, Panama City, Monterey.
- **Samuel's journal**: The private record Thomas's codes cannot hold. Color of water, sound of Lily's violin, how Clara walks at 4 AM. Blank space where the ambush explanation should go (ch-09).
- **Clara**: Has absorbed Harper's duties. Walks pre-dawn watch (4-6 AM). Coat folded for first time in ch-12. Reading the approaching coast.
- **Lily**: Just told Samuel about Harper's teaching (ch-13). "Thank you" — two words. The measurement system now applied to people.
