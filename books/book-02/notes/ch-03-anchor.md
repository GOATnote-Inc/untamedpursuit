# Compaction Anchor — Book 2, Chapter 3

## Chapter
Book 2, Chapter 3 — "Before Dawn"

## POV
Clara Chen. Exhausted — hasn't slept since the ambush, ears still ringing, back shaped by hours of surgery. She is holding the room together through operational discipline, but the foundation is moving beneath her. Harper's death is not a surprise. It is the arrival of a fact she has refused to schedule.

## Outline Beats
1. Harper dies in the hour before dawn. Clara is beside him. George at the foot of the bed, head down. The pipe tobacco has gone cold.
2. "Miss Chen." His last words. Not "Clara." In death he returns to what he was — her protector, formal, faithful. Clara: "Mr. Harper."
3. The burial. Above the city, facing the Pacific — the ocean Harper never crossed. Samuel digs. Lily places wildflowers. Clara puts the tobacco in the grave. George sits at the grave's edge and does not move until Clara signals him.
4. Clara stands looking west. Panama City below, the Pacific beyond. She has crossed half a continent and lost the man who kept her safe for four years. The question the book will answer begins here: what do you build when the person who kept you safe is gone?
5. Clara turns toward the harbor. The company must find a ship.

## Bible Facts Loaded
- Harper: David Harper, 40s. Dies ch-03, April 28. Wife Margaret (died of fever), daughter he never held, war in Florida. Last words: "Miss Chen." Buried above Panama City, facing the Pacific. Pipe tobacco in the grave.
- Clara: Canton training (Dr. Murray). Architectural metaphor domain. Shoulder rig. Has not eaten since the trail. Back shaped by surgery. Ears ringing from the ambush.
- Samuel: Merchant metaphor domain. Mother-hollow thread (Front Street, Wexford docks). First combat use of Colt Paterson yesterday. Standing watch outside Harper's door.
- Lily: Violin, artist. Wissahickon Creek. Natural/sensory metaphor domain. Plays through the wall. Wildflowers at the grave.
- George: 140 lbs, silent, hand signals only. Responds to 12 commands. Will sit at the grave's edge and not move until Clara signals.
