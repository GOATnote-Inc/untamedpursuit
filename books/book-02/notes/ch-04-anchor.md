# Compaction Anchor — Book 2, Chapter 4

## Chapter
Book 2, Chapter 4 — "The Fever"

## POV
Samuel Taylor. First POV chapter in Book 2. He dug Harper's grave hours/days ago. Standing watch with an empty pistol was his instinctive response to Harper's dying — he took the post without knowing the rotation. Now he is processing grief through commerce and practicality: counting what they have, managing what they need, applying his merchant's arithmetic to survival. His metaphor domain is commercial/mercantile — ledgers, cargo, balance sheets, manifests, the arithmetic of value and cost.

## Outline Beats
1. Cholera in Panama City. The port thick with it — contaminated water, crowded transit housing, tropical heat. Clara channels grief into medicine, treating strangers as though saving them might undo what she couldn't prevent.
2. Samuel watches Clara work. She moves like Harper now — scanning rooms, checking exits, positioning George. She has absorbed his habits without noticing.
3. Two crew fall ill. Clara quarantines them. Samuel manages provisions — merchant's arithmetic applied to water rations, quinine stores, distance to the harbor.
4. Samuel's private grief for Harper. The flowers backstage at the Golden Compass. Harper's nod when Samuel checked his pistol on the trail. The handoff already made — Samuel just didn't know he was receiving it.
5. Reyes brings intelligence: a ship in the harbor, the Resolute, bound for California. Captain willing to take passengers.

## Bible Facts Loaded
- Samuel: 30s, powerful build, calloused hands. Boxing-trained. Father was Irish dock worker from Wexford. First combat use of Colt Paterson April 26. Merchant metaphor domain. "A businessman who knows when words are inadequate." Weeps openly when moved. Candid, self-aware, direct.
- Clara: Channeling grief into operational control. Canton medical training. Architectural metaphor domain. By now has absorbed Harper's watch rotation, hand signals, room-reading habits.
- Lily: Artist, violin, Wissahickon Creek. Natural/sensory metaphor domain. Played violin through the wall in ch-02.
- Reyes: Trail scout, local knowledge. Cleared the path on the way to the burial.
- George: 140 lbs, silent, hand signals. Now responds to Clara's commands after the transfer at the grave.
- Harper (memory): Presented Samuel with flowers backstage at the Golden Compass. Nodded when Samuel checked his pistol.
