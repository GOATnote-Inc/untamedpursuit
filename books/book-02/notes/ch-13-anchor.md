# Compaction Anchor — Book 2, Chapter 13

## Chapter: Book 2, Chapter 13
- **Title**: "What He Taught Her"
- **POV**: Lily Montgomery — second POV chapter. Shifting from solitary observation (ch-11, painting sunsets) to interpersonal vulnerability (telling Samuel about Harper's teaching). Measurement filter now applies to people, not light.
- **Timeline**: ~May 26--28, 1847
- **Location**: Aboard the Resolute, Pacific Ocean (foredeck, early morning)

## Outline Beats
1. Samuel asked about Harper's teaching. Lily has been waiting for someone to ask.
2. The telling costs Lily — every detail brings the hold back. The hold is the context; the teaching is the answer.
3. Teaching sequence: rifle (distance) -> knife (when distance fails) -> dog (Ajax, the companion who chooses you) -> "He wanted me to choose safety. But he also wanted me to know that I could choose the thing that wasn't safe."
4. Why she tells Samuel: she has measured him and found him worthy. He wept at Clara's music, dug Harper's grave, carries a gun like Clara now. Second facet of measurement system — trust, measuring people.
5. Samuel's response: "Thank you." Two words. Does not interrupt or offer comfort. Exactly right. Lily knows she chose correctly.

## Bible Facts Loaded
- **Lily**: Natural/artistic/sensory metaphor domain. Hold = zero on her scale. Measures rooms (9x6x5, door opens from inside?). Paints sunsets as evidence against darkness. "Not out of fear. Out of choice." Requested weapons training + dog training. Ajax "already chose" Lily. Wissahickon Creek orchard — Rhode Island Greenings, Northern Spys, Baldwins.
- **Samuel**: Merchant/commercial metaphors. Candid, direct, comfortable with silence. Weeps openly when moved. Father from Wexford, Irish dock worker. Mother Mary — died when Samuel was 17 (lungs). First combat use of Paterson Colt at mule-trail ambush. Dug Harper's grave (ch-03). Two records: coded letters to Thomas, private journal.
- **Harper**: David Harper. "Miss Chen" always. First day 1843: "Where do you need me" / "Everywhere." Watched Clara three days before presenting himself. Wife Margaret (died of fever), daughter never met. Pipe — Virginia leaf. Taught Lily at Wissahickon: rifle first, knife second, Ajax placed. Taught by seasons, patience, silence between lessons. Died ch-03, buried above Panama City facing Pacific.
- **Voice study key points**: Lily's voice = painting (reaching for something true). Colors, not rooms. Growth, not architecture. Hold appears as lens, not wound — "six days" is the euphemism. Short sentences for impact, long for immersion. No military/commercial/legal metaphors.
