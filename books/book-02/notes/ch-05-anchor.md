# Compaction Anchor — Book 2, Chapter 5

## Chapter
Book 2, Chapter 5 — "The Resolute"

## POV
Samuel Taylor. He has just negotiated passage. He is now writing the second coded letter to Thomas — Panama City, the second of three ports. He must decide what to include and what to withhold. His merchant mind is weighing information like cargo.

## Outline Beats
1. Samuel negotiates passage on the Resolute. Captain Morales — weathered coastal trader, practical, unsentimental. Samuel uses merchant language. The price is steep. Samuel pays without haggling — the arithmetic of survival.
2. Samuel writes a coded letter to Thomas. Three ports: Kingston, Panama City, Monterey. Thomas's system — the wax, the cipher, the routing. Samuel writes what happened: the ambush, Harper's death, cholera. He does NOT write what he suspects: that the ambush was organized by someone who knew their route.
3. Loading the ship. The company diminished — crew dead from cholera, the rest weakened. Lily helps load supplies. Clara supervises with George at her side, occupying Harper's position.
4. The letter sealed and left with a shipping agent. Thomas will receive it in six weeks. Samuel holds the cipher key and wonders what Thomas will do with the intelligence.

## Bible Facts Loaded
- Samuel: merchant metaphor domain, direct, candid. First combat Paterson. Father was Wexford dock worker.
- Captain Morales: new character — weathered coastal trader, practical, unsentimental. Sailed the coast: Monterey to San Francisco.
- Thomas: oldest friend, intelligence chief, writes manifest with own name absent, manages Clara's affairs. Correspondence codes — wax, cipher, routing. Three ports.
- Clara: now occupying Harper's position. Leather coat, Colts, George at her side.
- Lily: helping load supplies, steady and quiet.

## Previous Chapter Landing
Ch-04 ended with Samuel walking toward the harbor at dusk. "The company was smaller than it had been. The ledger was shorter. But the accounts were not closed." Reyes had found the Resolute. Clara gave Samuel the negotiation role.

## Threads Active
- Thomas correspondence codes (Samuel uses the system but withholds suspicion)
- Informed ambush (Samuel suspects but doesn't write it)
- Voyage preparation
- Samuel's merchant competence as survival tool
