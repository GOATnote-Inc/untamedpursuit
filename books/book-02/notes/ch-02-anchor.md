# Compaction Anchor — Book 2, Chapter 2

## Chapter
Book 2, Chapter 2 — "Panama City"

## POV
Clara Chen. In shock beneath operational control. She just carried a dying man six miles through jungle. Ears still ringing from the ambush. She knows the perforated bowel diagnosis means death deferred, not prevented. She will not accept it. She operates anyway.

## Outline Beats
1. Panama City at dusk. Clara commandeers a room above a cantina. Harper on the table. Clara works with what she has — Canton training, field supplies, lamp burning low.
2. The surgery. Perforated bowel. In 1847, this is death deferred. She operates anyway. Her hands do what her mind won't accept.
3. The night conversation. Harper awake, clear-eyed. Pipe tobacco — Clara brought it from Philadelphia. For the first time, they speak without "Miss Chen." Harper reveals: wife who died young, daughter he never met, a war he came home from but never left. He chose Clara's service because protecting someone worth protecting made the rest bearable.
4. Harper's instruction: watch rotation, protocols, signals George knows. Passing the manual. Not because she needs it — she built the system. Because someone needs to hold what he held.
5. Samuel sits outside the door. Lily plays violin softly in the next room — first music since the ambush.

## Bible Facts Loaded
- Harper: David Harper, 40s, weathered, loyal eyes. Always "Miss Chen." Dies ch-03, April 28. Wife died young, daughter never met, war veteran. Deepest fear: failing Clara.
- Clara: Canton Missionary Hospital 3 years (age ~19, Dr. Murray). Perforated bowel = "a schedule." Shoulder rig. Sword cane. Architectural metaphor domain.
- Lily: Violin, cello. Wissahickon Creek. Six days in hold. Ajax chose her. Preserves Harper's memory.
- Samuel: Knows when words are inadequate. Pistol at hip. Merchant metaphor domain.
- George: 140 lbs, silent, hand signals, attacks without command when needed.

## Previous Chapter Landing
Ch-01 ended with Clara walking beside Harper's litter through six miles of jungle. She did not let go of the rail. Did not stop giving orders. Did not allow the structure of her competence to show the crack forming in its foundation. Her ears ringing from gunfire. Physical cost present: pressure behind eyes, settling weapons by feel.
