# Continuity Report — Book 2, Chapter 13

## Significant Error

**Training origin contradicts book-01, ch-17.** The draft conflates two separate moments:
- **Draft**: Lily went to Clara, said "Not out of fear. Out of choice," and Harper came the next morning.
- **Canon** (book-01-timeline.md, line 119): Clara rides to Wissahickon Creek and *discovers* Harper has been training Lily since autumn. Harper trained Lily without Clara's knowledge — the first time in four years he acted independently. The phrase "Not out of fear. Out of choice" was spoken when Lily asked to join the voyage west, not when requesting training.
- **Impact**: Two separate canonical moments are merged into one. The scene must be rewritten to reflect Harper's independent initiative and the correct context for Lily's defining phrase.
