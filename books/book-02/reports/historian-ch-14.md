# Historian Report — Book 2, Chapter 14

## Anachronisms

- **"Network" ×4** — organizational sense banned (20th c.). Replaced with "apparatus" and "correspondence chain."
- **"Intelligence reporting"** — professionalized compound (20th c.). Replaced with "coded correspondence."
- **"Grandfather clock"** — already ruled accepted for readability (ch-02). Removed in Thomas section compression; no action needed.

## Geographic Correction

- **Three inlets approaching Monterey** — Monterey Bay is a single broad bay, not three separate inlets. Fixed via Samuel's miscount: he sees three, corrects to two (shadow between ridgelines).

## Wind Direction Note

- **Northwest wind + sage scent** — Northwest wind blows from ocean, cannot carry land scents to ship. Morales's "offshore wind" changed to "when the wind was right" to remove the contradiction.
