# Style Review: Book 2, Chapter 8

## Issues Found and Fixed

### "The way" constructions: 6 → 2
- KEPT: "The way one registered a load-bearing wall" (Clara domain, strongest use)
- KEPT: "Lily carried the way Samuel carried a manifest" (character-comparative work)
- CUT: "the way a building could be held" → restructured with em-dash
- CUT: "the way a clock continued" → appositive
- CUT: "She saw it in the way the rhythms" → filter + construction removed
- CUT: "in the way her body thought" → restructured

### "[Noun] who" clauses: 11+ → ~6
- Restructured Morales "whose arithmetic," Morales "who had carried," Cole's two instances, Jennings "who did not argue," Samuel "who was learning," Clara self-awareness "who deceived herself"

### Other fixes applied
- "Act I's grief" → "the first watches after Panama" (fictional frame break)
- "Grief's arithmetic" → "The weight compounded daily" (domain fix: arithmetic → structural)
- "Body's arithmetic" → cut (Murray restatement removed)
- Self-awareness flattery cut ("She was not a woman who deceived herself")
- Domesticity label → "a rhythm Clara had no architecture for" (domain-appropriate)
- "The particular" tic: 4 → 1
- Triple "specific/specificity" → varied ("given," "precise," "rigor")
- Filter words: 5 → ~1
- Exit structure differentiated (cut "The ship moved west. The coat stayed on." → end on "His eyes on the door.")
- Tricolon duplication varied (line 39 merged into flowing sentence)

## Strengths
- Opening line outstanding
- George's waiting (lines 33-35) devastating and economical
- Clara-Lily dialogue distinct and Bechdel-passing
- Coat-button section emotionally precise
