## POV Discipline

No POV violations. Strict third-person limited through Samuel throughout. The journal-within-chapter structure (italicized entries) is Samuel's own words and stays within his perspective. Morales conversation rendered through Samuel's perception. Lily's violin heard through the wall — Samuel does not claim to know what Lily is feeling, only what the music sounds like to him.

No tense slips. Past tense maintained throughout prose narration. Journal entries in italics use present/past naturally as journal writing would.

## Dialogue

## Pattern Analysis

### "The way" constructions
- **Count (pre-revision)**: 4
- **Ceiling**: 2
- **Instances found**:
  1. Line 86: "the way he read a market" — Samuel's commercial domain, load-bearing
  2. Line 117: "the way they had practiced in Philadelphia" — back-reference, functional
  3. Line 50: "the way warmth traveled through a well-built wall" — architectural domain (Clara's, not Samuel's)
  4. Line 154: "the way you trusted a warehouse floor" — commercial domain, load-bearing
- **Recommendation**: Cut #3 (domain mismatch — "well-built wall" is Clara's architectural lens, not Samuel's commercial lens). Keep #1 and #4 (strongest commercial domain examples). Cut or rework #2.

### "[Noun] who" clauses
- **Count (pre-revision)**: 12
- **Ceiling**: 6
- **Heaviest concentration**: Lines 34, 50-52 (Spanish book), 70-71 (Morales approach), 82-83, 106, 138-142 (Thomas section), 163-164 (violin), 168-170 (finale triple)
- **Recommendation**: Cut 6. Prioritize cutting: generic characterizations (lines 70-71 Morales as "man who"), repetitive constructions in the Thomas section, and the triple "[noun] who" at the finale.

### Admiration ratio
- **Samuel observing others**: Morales 4/4 admiring (experienced captain, wise merchant, accurate navigator, shrewd observer). Zero complications, zero friction, zero moments where Morales's competence fails or misleads.
- **Recommendation**: Add one Morales complication — a warning that qualifies his commercial optimism, or a moment where his coast knowledge reveals a limitation.

### Banned vocabulary
- **"assessment"** (line 106): "The look held a merchant's assessment"
- **Ruling**: Change to "appraisal" — more period-specific and within Samuel's commercial domain.
- No other banned words found (protocol, shifts, network, infrastructure, blueprint, processed, operational, rehydration — all absent).
