# Historian Report: Book 2, Chapter 1

## Anachronisms Fixed

- **"kill zone"** (3 occurrences) — 20th century military term (FM 31-20, 1965). Replaced with "killing ground."
- **"sightlines"** (1 occurrence) — on banned list. Replaced with "lines of sight."
- **"shooters"** (3 occurrences) — on banned list. Replaced with "marksmen" / "the last man."

## Historical Issue Fixed

- **Dr. Parker** renamed to **Dr. Murray** — the real surgeon at Canton Hospital was Peter Parker (American, Massachusetts). A fictional Scottish "Dr. Parker" at the same institution creates confusion. Murray is common Scottish, no character collision.

## Deliberate Anachronism Noted

- **"shoulder rig"** — earliest shoulder holster (Texas Pattern) dates to late 1870s. Already in Book 1 canon as "Harper adapted for the crossing." Needs formal `[deliberate anachronism]` ruling.

Camino de Cruces dressed stones, escopeta, Colt Paterson five-shot, percussion caps, perforated bowel as fatal (87% mortality even in Civil War), mule train transport, litter construction, jungle conditions, black powder smoke behavior.
