# Continuity Report — Book 2, Chapter 15

## Errors (must fix)

- **[Flower colors at Harper's grave]**: Ch-15 says Lily sees wildflowers "yellow and white, the same colors she had placed on Harper's grave" (line 65). In ch-03, the flowers Lily placed were three colors: "yellow and white and a deep violet" (ch-03, line 112; also line 142: "Yellow and white and violet against the wool"). The violet is omitted, making "the same colors" inaccurate.

## Warnings (review needed)

- **[Clara's coat status]**: Leather coat "buttoned to the collar against the morning cold" (line 61). In ch-12 (~May 22-25), Clara folded the coat across the chair for the first time (Thread 11 disarmament milestone). By ch-15 (~June 5-7, two weeks later), coat is back on. Plausible (ch-12 removal was private, music-prompted), but author should confirm intentional rearming.

- **["She loves him"]**: Lily says of Clara and Thomas: "Because she loves him. And love does not measure" (line 175). Bible is explicit that Clara/Thomas are "never a love interest — a friend, but a jealous one." Clara says "I love you, Thomas" in book-01, ch-02 (friendship-love). Lily's use reads as friendship-love in context, but could mislead readers.

- **[Company whereabouts]**: Only Lily, Samuel, Clara, George, and Ajax at Monterey. Reyes, Cole, Jennings not referenced. Acceptable for focused POV but their continued presence should be acknowledged somewhere nearby.
