# Historian Report — Book 2, Chapter 13

## Error

**Apple ripening order reversed.** Lines 85 and 99 state Northern Spys ripen first, then Greenings, then Baldwins. The actual ripening order in a northeastern U.S. orchard:
1. Rhode Island Greening — late September to early October
2. Baldwin — mid-to-late October
3. Northern Spy — late October to November

Northern Spy is the *latest* of the three to ripen, not the earliest. The chapter has the order reversed. Two passages affected.

Also: "Northern Spys" should be "Northern Spies" (standard plural of the variety name).
