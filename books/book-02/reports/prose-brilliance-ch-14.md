# Prose Brilliance Report — Book 2, Chapter 14

## Competencies: 1 tested, 3 confirmed

The "could not" / "would not" correction is the chapter's only genuine test — Samuel catching himself in a self-serving framing. The rest confirms: he counts, journals, suspects Thomas, declines to act. No competence meets resistance.

## Admiration Ratio: ~1.2:1

Close to healthy. Worst offender: blade/hilt metaphor — asserted partnership without friction or cost. If deleted, Clara's and Lily's qualities would still be visible through action (chart-studying, painting).

## Morales Competence Trap

"Charts were always accurate because Morales did not keep charts that weren't" — infallible wallpaper. Needs one gap or uncertainty.

## Metaphor Domain

Strong overall (3:1 domain-specific to generic). Three leaks:
- "Brushstroke of brown and green" (line 46) — Lily's domain
- "Morning fog dissolves" (line 104) — generic
- "In waves, each wave stronger" (line 132) — generic

## Samuel Misreading

## Exit Structure

"California." — single word, effective punch. Different enough from prior chapter endings. The next Samuel chapter should exit with action or dialogue.
