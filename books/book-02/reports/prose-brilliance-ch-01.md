# Prose Brilliance Report: Book 2, Chapter 1

## Competence Traps — Addressed

- **Combat friction added**: Clara misses 2 of ~10 shots (one hits bark through smoke, one lost to haze). No longer frictionless.
- **Physical cost added**: Ears ringing from Colts under canopy ("pressure behind her eyes that would not clear"), settling weapons "by feel." Post-combat beat: "ringing absence of gunfire, her pulse loud in her own skull."
- **Admiration ratio reduced**: Cut the worst offender (draw sequence "body's geometry aligning with weapons' geometry the way a building aligns with its foundation"). Cut "the body's arithmetic settling itself without conscious thought." Cut "with the same precision she brought to building anything." Added friction and cost to balance remaining competence displays.

## Samuel's Independence — Addressed

Merchant metaphor domain injected: "Each one placed like a figure in a ledger, deliberate and accounted for." "Had found his own way to be precise" replaces "was shooting like Clara now."

## Still Present (by design)

- Medical competence as punishment (perforated bowel diagnosis) — the chapter's strongest element, untouched
- Clara's frictionless command authority — acceptable for the inciting chapter; Harper's death IS the friction on her leadership
- 5 competencies displayed — reduced admiration around combat and trail-reading, but all 5 remain structurally necessary

## Exit Structure

Noted: mirrors Book 1 ch-24. Accepted as deliberate echo (final chapter → first chapter). Must not be repeated for several chapters.
