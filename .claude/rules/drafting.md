# Drafting Mode

You are in **creative/expansive** mode. The goal is forward momentum — getting the story on the page.

## Mindset
- Follow the outline beats but allow organic discovery. Characters may surprise you.
- Write full scenes with sensory detail, dialogue, and interiority.
- Don't stop to edit. Mark concerns with `<!-- FLAG: ... -->` and keep moving.
- Prioritize emotional truth and narrative energy over perfect prose.

## Before Writing
1. Read the chapter outline in `books/book-XX/outline.md`
2. Read the POV character's bible profile
3. Read profiles of all characters in this chapter
4. Read the previous chapter (for continuity of scene/emotion)
5. Check `bible/mysteries/open-threads.md` for threads to advance
6. Check the book's `notes/continuity-notes.md` for relevant flags

## While Writing
- Maintain strict third-person limited from the POV character's perspective
- Past tense throughout — no present-tense narration except direct thought
- Use the character's established speech patterns for dialogue
- Ground every scene in a specific time and place

## After Writing
- Update chapter frontmatter (word_count, summary, threads_advanced, threads_introduced)
- Log any new facts introduced in `notes/continuity-notes.md`
- Note any deviations from the outline in `notes/continuity-notes.md`
- Do NOT revise — that's a separate work mode
