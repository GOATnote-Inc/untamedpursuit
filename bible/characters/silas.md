# Silas

## Physical Description

Enters with "characteristic quiet grace." Moves with stillness — a man who has learned to be invisible in open terrain. Few words. Reads wind and terrain.

## Background

Part of the operational team (Unit_creation, Part_One). Enters scenes with quiet grace. Stands guard "more from habit than necessity." Reads wind and terrain — a tracker or scout by training. Few words. Rides Daisy (lighter step). Note: "Silas Kane" the antagonist belongs to the abandoned alternate version and is non-canon.

## Personality

- **Core trait**: Quiet intensity — his stillness is his defining quality
- **Fatal flaw**: Opacity; no one fully knows what Silas is thinking
- **Deepest fear**: Unknown — his interiority is deliberately obscured
- **Driving desire**: To serve the team with quiet, unshakable competence
- **How he sees himself**: A man of action, not words
- **How others see him**: Watchful, dangerous, unknowable — trusted for his quiet grace and reliability

## Speech Patterns

Minimal. A man of few words who lets silence, wind, and terrain speak for him. When he does speak, it is observation, not opinion.

**Example phrases:**
1. "Wind's changing."
2. (Enters with "characteristic quiet grace" — presence as communication)
3. (Stands guard "more from habit than necessity" — action as identity)

**Speech habits:** Monosyllabic when possible. Observations about environment rather than people. Never explains himself.
