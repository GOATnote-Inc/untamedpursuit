# Kate Garrett

## Physical Description

Carries herself with a surgeon's posture — straight, precise, no wasted motion. Hands that have saved lives and held rifles with equal steadiness. Expert rifle shot. Her appearance communicates competence before she speaks a word.

## Background

Daughter of Boston's chief of surgery. Born Katherine Blackwood. Trained at the Woman's Medical College of Pennsylvania — one of the few women to receive formal medical education in this era. First met Clara Chen in an anatomy lab during medical training (~early 1850s), forging a lifelong bond. Married Jack Garrett after the 1860 Philadelphia riot, where they met. Practiced as both a surgeon and frontier doctor, treating all patients regardless of race. Expert marksman in addition to her medical skills. Alive at the time of Clara's death. Killed alongside Jack on the train during the Gatling gun ambush (Iron_and_Blood), which occurs later.

## Personality

- **Core trait**: Exacting precision — in surgery, in speech, in judgment
- **Fatal flaw**: Impatience with those who do not meet her standards
- **Deepest fear**: Failing a patient when it matters most
- **Driving desire**: To practice medicine with integrity, regardless of circumstance or prejudice
- **How she sees herself**: A surgeon who thinks, not a medical student who reads
- **How others see her**: Sharp, no-nonsense, brilliant — "Smart woman, your mother" (Jack)

## Speech Patterns

Precise Boston diction. Clipped, efficient, no-nonsense. Teaches through pattern recognition and directed observation rather than lecture. Commands respect without raising her voice. Her accent sharpens when she is worried or angry.

**Example phrases:**
1. "I need surgeons who can think, not medical students who can read."
2. "Less talking, more dissecting."
3. "Everything has a purpose. Everything connects."

**Speech habits:** Short declarative sentences. Imperative mood when teaching. Dislikes repetition — says things once and expects them to land. Boston accent intensifies under stress.
