# Jessie

## Physical Description

Built for action — a former bounty hunter's physique. Carries herself with brash confidence. Has a "trademark smirk" that signals both amusement and defiance. Rides Queen, a black, elegant, lethal horse that matches her temperament.

## Background

Former bounty hunter who became Eve's best friend and operational partner. 10 years of friendship with Eve. 7 years of training with Clara Chen, suggesting she entered Clara's orbit in her late teens or early twenties. Earned Storm's trust over 3 years — a significant marker of her patience and character given Storm's fierce selectivity. Took a bullet for Eve at some point during their partnership. Has a romantic relationship with Captain Andrews, a military officer stationed near Reno. Uses dynamite with enthusiasm and precision.

## Personality

- **Core trait**: Brash, irreverent loyalty — fierce devotion wrapped in sarcasm
- **Fatal flaw**: Recklessness; her confidence can tip into overreach
- **Deepest fear**: That she will not be enough when Eve truly needs her
- **Driving desire**: Something bigger than bounty hunting — purpose, belonging, family
- **How she sees herself**: The one who gets things done while everyone else talks
- **How others see her**: Irreverent, sexually confident, dangerous, utterly dependable

## Speech Patterns

Casual, brash, irreverent. Humor is her default register — deflects with wit, commits with action. Sexually confident in her language. Uses "coin for your thoughts" instead of the common "penny." Direct but never cruel. Her affection for Eve surfaces through teasing.

**Example phrases:**
1. "You can be so needy."
2. "I'm in. Been looking for something bigger than bounty hunting."
3. "A coin for your thoughts."

**Speech habits:** Teasing as affection. Dark humor under pressure. Says "First was SPECIAL. Second was pure art. Third was just because." about her dynamite use — escalating irreverence as her signature. Never apologizes for who she is.
