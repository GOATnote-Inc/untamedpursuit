# Storm

## Physical Description

Big mare. Fierce, loyal, intelligent. "Theatrical indifference" as her default expression — a horse with personality and presence. Alert and watchful beneath the apparent disinterest. Disliked trains. Chose Eve as her rider — "Takes a saddle just fine. She just chooses who puts it on."

## Background

Eve Garrett's primary horse. A big mare defined by fierce loyalty and selective trust. Chose Eve — the relationship was initiated by the horse, not the rider. Jessie earned Storm's trust over 3 years, indicating how rarely Storm accepts anyone besides Eve. Disliked trains, which is notable given the train attack that defines the series' pivotal trauma. Her theatrical indifference masks deep alertness and intelligence. Has a foal mentioned in the epilogue of the 4-act outline.

**Other horses of note:**
- **Queen** — Jessie's horse. Black, elegant, lethal. Matches Jessie's temperament.
- **Daisy** — Silas's horse. Lighter step. Matches Silas's quiet grace.
- **Storm's foal** — Mentioned in epilogue of 4-act outline. Legacy and continuity.
