# Ghost

## Physical Description

Grey coat. Appears as a pup in some scenes and as an adult in others — timeline placement determines his age. Combat-trained. Carries "Jack's steady patience" in his bearing — calm, watchful, reliable. Takes point and provides flank security in operational contexts.

## Background

Originally Jack Garrett's dog, then Eve's after Jack's death on the train (Iron_and_Blood). Howled for 3 days after the train attack — his grief mirrors Eve's. Combat-trained, functioning as both companion and tactical asset. Takes point position and provides flank security, indicating advanced military or operational dog training. His transition from Jack to Eve parallels Eve's inheritance of her father's Winchester and her mother's medical bag.
