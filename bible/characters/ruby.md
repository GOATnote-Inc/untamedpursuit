# Ruby

## Physical Description

Golden Retriever. Functions as scout and moral compass. Maps approach routes. Judges character — "Ears prick forward when she trusts people." Alert, intelligent, warm. A Golden Retriever's temperament applied to operational work.

## Background

Jack Garrett's Golden Retriever. Functions beyond companionship as a scout who maps approach routes and a judge of character whose reactions inform the team's trust assessments. Her ears pricking forward when she trusts people makes her an instinctive security asset. Embodies Jack's own qualities — warm, steady, perceptive — in canine form.
