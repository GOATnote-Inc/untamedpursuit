# George

## Physical Description

Massive black dog, approximately 140 pounds — a Newfoundland cross with continental European stock (likely Swiss Metzgerhund or Sennenhund breeding), acquired as a puppy through Clara's Pacific trading network. No established English breed name in the 1840s; characters refer to him descriptively ("Clara's black dog") or by name. Intelligent, silent, operates entirely with hand signals. Described as "an avenging spirit." Attacks with surgical precision — not feral violence but trained, deliberate force. Despite his combat capability, provides comfort to rescued children. `[deliberate anachronism — in-story justification: rare European import via trading network, no established breed name in period. Newfoundland base provides the intelligence, loyalty, and maritime association; continental cross provides the protection-breed temperament and combat capability.]`

## Background

Clara Chen's dog, acquired as a puppy through her Canton trading connections from a European breeder — possibly Swiss or German. European dog breeds moved along the same trading routes as goods and people. Trained to operate on hand signals — no verbal commands, making him silent and effective in covert operations. Functions as both companion and security asset. His dual nature — avenging spirit who comforts children — mirrors Clara's own duality as warrior and protector. Part of Clara's dog training program, which also produced Ajax.
