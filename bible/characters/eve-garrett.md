# Eve Garrett

## Physical Description

Green eyes. Red hair. Wears a wide-brimmed hat and shawl. "Surgeon's hands, shooter's hands" -- hands shaped by both medical training and firearms proficiency. Captivated by bright colors of fabrics and practical gear.

## Background

Daughter of Kate Garrett (medical doctor) and Jack Garrett (Pony Express rider turned marshal). Spent summers at Clara Chen's Monterey estate from age 5.

Trained by three mentors:
- **Kate Garrett (mother)**: Medicine, anatomy, surgical precision.
- **Jack Garrett (father)**: Winchester marksmanship, shooting, tracking, "reading air" -- a tracking/sensing skill unique to Jack's teaching.
- **Clara Chen (mentor)**: Sword, Japanese martial arts, Chinese medicine, European fencing, American frontier tactics, philosophy, languages, Latin, sign language.

Became a physician (Dr. Eve Garrett). Parents killed in a Gatling gun ambush on a train (Iron_and_Blood). After their deaths, carries her mother's medical bag and her father's Winchester.

## Personality

- **Core trait**: Pattern recognition -- sees connections others miss.
- **Fatal flaw**: Emotionally restrained; holds grief too tightly.
- **Deepest fear**: Losing the small memories of her parents. "Not the big moments. But the small ones."
- **Driving desire**: To honor Clara's legacy while making it her own.
- **How they see themselves**: A reluctant inheritor, still earning the right to lead.
- **How others see them**: "You have fire" (Clara). Smart, kind, fierce.

## Speech Patterns

Restrained and precise. Medical vocabulary mixed with frontier directness. Philosophical when contemplative. Occasionally profane. Uses Latin -- "Homo sum, humani nihil a me alienum puto." Fluent in sign language (learned from Clara).

**Example phrases:**
1. "You taught me everything I know, Clara. And not just about fighting or surviving. You've taught me how to live."
2. "Can I tell you stories that are meaningless? Just so my family's memories don't fade into sunsets?"
3. "When do we start?" / Clara: "My dear, you started years ago."

**Speech habits:** Tends toward economy of words. Defaults to medical precision in descriptions. Shifts into philosophical register during quiet, reflective moments. Latin phrases surface in moments of conviction. Communicates in sign language with Clara and later with others. Profanity is rare but deliberate.
