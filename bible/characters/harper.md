# David Harper

## Physical Description

40s. Weathered face with loyal eyes — a man whose body and expression have been shaped by years of vigilance. Carries himself with quiet professionalism. Not flashy; his presence is felt through attentiveness, not display.

## Background

Clara Chen's security chief. Devoted to Clara's safety with a professional deference that never wavers — addresses her as "Miss Chen" throughout. Resigned to Clara's independence and her insistence on taking personal risks, though he never stops trying to protect her. Follows Clara in her shadows when she ventures into danger. Escorts Irish workers as part of the network's operations. Presents Samuel Taylor with flowers backstage at the Golden Compass. Dies during the voyage west — Clara loses her security chief before reaching San Francisco.

## Personality

- **Core trait**: Quiet, unwavering loyalty
- **Fatal flaw**: Resignation — accepts Clara's risks rather than challenging her more forcefully
- **Deepest fear**: That he will fail Clara at the moment it matters most
- **Driving desire**: To keep Clara safe — a task he knows is impossible but pursues anyway
- **How he sees himself**: A man with a duty
- **How others see him**: Professional, gentle, quietly heroic — Lily preserves his memory through stories of kindness

## Speech Patterns

Professional and restrained. Quiet concern underlies every word. Deferential — "Miss Chen" is his default address. Does not argue; he advises, then follows. His silence often communicates more than his words.

**Example phrases:**
1. "Miss Chen..." (with quiet concern, the unfinished sentence saying everything)
2. (Presents flowers to Samuel backstage — actions over words)
3. (Follows Clara in shadows — duty expressed through presence, not speech)

**Speech habits:** Formal address ("Miss Chen"). Sentences trail off with unspoken worry. Professional vocabulary. Never raises his voice. Communicates devotion through action and proximity rather than declaration.
