# Jack Garrett

## Physical Description

Built for the frontier — a rider's body, lean and weathered. Smells of gun oil and sage. Uses a Winchester rifle as his primary weapon. Carries himself with quiet, steady warmth. His hands are sure whether holding reins, a rifle, or his daughter.

## Background

Pony Express rider (1860-1861) who later became a marshal. Both are canon at different life stages. Met Kate during the 1860 Philadelphia riot and married her afterward. Completed 14 missions for Samuel Taylor over 7 years as part of Clara's intelligence network. Trained Eve in shooting and tracking, including his signature skill of "reading air" — sensing wind, terrain, and threat through instinct honed by years on the frontier. Has a Golden Retriever named Ruby. Plans a "defensible base of operations" down south. Distrusts Thomas. Killed on the train during the Gatling gun ambush (Iron_and_Blood).

## Personality

- **Core trait**: Steady warmth — the calm center in any storm
- **Fatal flaw**: Trusts his instincts so deeply he may dismiss others' judgment (distrusts Thomas despite network loyalty)
- **Deepest fear**: Failing to protect his family
- **Driving desire**: To raise Eve to be capable and good, and to build something safe and lasting
- **How he sees himself**: A man doing quiet, necessary work
- **How others see him**: Warm, humorous, dependable — "the kind of man you'd trust with your life without being asked" (implied)

## Speech Patterns

Warm, laconic, unhurried. Teaches through silence as much as speech — lets Eve figure things out before offering guidance. Humor is dry and affectionate. Speaks about Kate with quiet reverence. Uses few words but each one counts.

**Example phrases:**
1. "You're going to have off days."
2. "Smart woman, your mother."
3. (Teaches through silence — demonstrates, waits, lets Eve find the answer)

**Speech habits:** Short sentences. Comfortable pauses. Affectionate understatement. Refers to Kate with quiet pride. Never lectures — models behavior instead.
