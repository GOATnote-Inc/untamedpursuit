# Relationships

## Purpose

Track how characters relate to each other and how those relationships evolve across books. This is the connective tissue of the series.

## Rules

- **Update both sides.** When adding or modifying a relationship, update both characters' profiles AND the relationship map.
- **Track evolution.** Relationships change. Note the book/chapter where significant shifts occur.
- **Distinguish types.** Family, romantic, political, professional, adversarial — label clearly.
- **Note asymmetry.** How A sees B may differ from how B sees A. Capture both perspectives.
