# World-Building

## Purpose

This directory documents the physical, political, cultural, and linguistic world of the series. These files are reference material — consult them when writing scenes that involve travel, politics, daily life, or period-specific details.

## Files

- `locations.md` — Named places with descriptions, significance, and which books feature them
- `politics.md` — Power structures, factions, conflicts, and how they evolve across the series
- `culture.md` — Social norms, customs, class structures, religion, and daily rituals
- `technology-and-daily-life.md` — What exists, what doesn't, how people travel/eat/work/communicate
- `language-and-dialect.md` — Period vocabulary, regional speech differences, terms to use and avoid

## Rules

- Historical accuracy is paramount — mark any intentional deviations as `[deliberate anachronism]` with justification
- When a scene establishes new world-building details, update the relevant file
- Cross-reference with `research/` for sourced historical details
