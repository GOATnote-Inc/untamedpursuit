## Character Speech Patterns

<!-- How each major character sounds in dialogue -->

- **Clara**: Measured, proverbial, "river-stone smoothness." Shifts between formality and warmth depending on audience. Shifts between Cantonese and Mandarin accents depending on shipping contacts. Her speech carries quiet authority -- she rarely raises her voice because she never needs to.
- **Kate**: "Precise Boston diction" that sharpens when she is worried or angry. Clinical vocabulary bleeds into everyday speech. Formal register even in casual settings.
- **Jessie**: Brash, informal, frontier vernacular, profane. Says exactly what she means with no diplomatic cushioning. Her speech is fast, direct, and often laced with dark humor.
- **Samuel**: To be documented as drafting reveals his voice.

## Regional Dialects

<!-- How speech varies by region, class, and education level -->

- **Philadelphia**: Urban, educated registers among Clara's circle. Dock workers speak differently from townhouse society.
- **Boston**: Kate's origin -- precise, clipped, formal diction.
- **Frontier / Colorado Territory**: Informal, practical, stripped of Eastern formality. Jessie exemplifies this register.
- **Cantonese / Mandarin influence**: Clara code-switches accents depending on which shipping contacts she is addressing.

## Terms of Address

<!-- How characters address each other based on rank, familiarity, and social context -->

- **Formal**: "Miss Chen," "Dr. Garrett," "Mr. Taylor" -- used in public, professional, or first-meeting contexts
- **Military**: "Captain Andrews" -- rank-based address
- **Professional title**: "Demolitions Specialist" (Jessie)
- Shift from formal to familiar address marks deepening trust between characters

## Private Communication Systems

- **Sign language**: Clara and Eve share a private sign language system -- not standard ASL but a personal code developed between them. Used for covert communication in public settings and for intimate conversation.
- **Hand signals for dogs**: George and Ghost respond to hand commands, not verbal ones. Adds tactical advantage in silent operations.
- **Coded messages**: Written communication within Clara's network uses codes to prevent interception.

## Profanity & Oaths

<!-- Period-appropriate exclamations, curses, and religious oaths -->

- Jessie is the most profane character; her language reflects frontier bluntness
- Clara almost never swears; when she does, it carries weight
- Period-appropriate oaths preferred over modern profanity
