# Timeline

## Format Rules

- `master-timeline.md` contains the full series chronology — every significant event across all 9 books
- `book-XX-timeline.md` files contain detailed per-book timelines with chapter-level granularity
- Use consistent date formatting: **Month Year** (e.g., "March 1347")
- Mark events as `[historical]` or `[fictional]` to distinguish real from invented
- When a chapter draft establishes a specific date, update the relevant timeline immediately
- Cross-reference character profiles when timeline entries involve character movements or life events

## Timeline Entry Format

```
### [Date or Date Range]
- **Event**: What happened
- **Location**: Where
- **Characters involved**: Who
- **Source**: book-XX/ch-XX or [historical]
- **Significance**: Why it matters to the story
```
