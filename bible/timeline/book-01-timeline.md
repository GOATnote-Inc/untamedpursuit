# Book 1 — Detailed Timeline

<!-- Chapter-level chronology for Book 1. -->
<!-- Set ~Autumn 1846, Philadelphia. Exact dates are approximate; sequence is canonical. -->

### Night of the Mercy Jane Rescue — Chapter 1
- **Event**: Clara's Night Watch operation rescues two girls (older ~10, younger ~5) from the trafficking ship Mercy Jane. Twelve-plus traffickers killed. Samuel Taylor intervenes during the rescue — first meeting between Clara and Samuel.
- **Location**: Philadelphia docks, Clara's private dock (northern end of the port)
- **Characters involved**: Clara, Samuel, Harper, George
- **POV**: Clara
- **Significance**: Inciting event of the book. Clara and Samuel meet in combat. Samuel had conducted 3 weeks of surveillance on the Mercy Jane before the rescue. Both men know each other's names before introduction — implies intelligence connections on both sides.

### The Following Morning — Chapter 2
- **Event**: Clara debriefs with Thomas Arlington in his ground-floor office. Thomas assesses Samuel from existing property records. Clara assigns Thomas to prepare a full report. "I love you, Thomas" / "And well, you should" exchange.
- **Location**: Thomas's office, Clara's townhouse
- **Characters involved**: Clara, Thomas, George
- **POV**: Clara
- **Significance**: Establishes Clara-Thomas dynamic. Thomas is trusted, central, "indispensable." First dual-frequency seeds planted.

### Dawn, ~One Day Later — Chapter 3
- **Event**: Clara practices weapons in her courtyard. Thomas delivers his report on Samuel. Clara and Samuel meet for tea at the Chestnut Street parlor — first real conversation. Samuel reveals Wallace's rejected partnership offer and connection to trafficking. Clara discloses a fraction of her shipping holdings. Handshake at the end.
- **Location**: Clara's courtyard, Chestnut Street parlor
- **Characters involved**: Clara, Samuel, Thomas, George (absent from tea — deliberate)
- **POV**: Clara
- **Significance**: Wallace named as antagonist. Samuel's background established (Irish father, ethical business philosophy). Clara's progressive disarmament continues (dark green silk, no leather coat, sword cane only).

### ~One Week After Chapter 1 — Chapter 4
- **Event**: Samuel attends the Golden Compass for Clara's performance. Clara plays cello, duets with Lily (violin), sings Italian arias, plays piano, performs original composition. Samuel weeps openly. Harper presents Samuel with flowers backstage. Lily introduced. George's head settles in Samuel's lap. Chapter ends at the threshold of the private dining room.
- **Location**: The Golden Compass music hall
- **Characters involved**: Samuel, Clara, Lily, Harper, George
- **POV**: Samuel (first Samuel POV chapter)
- **Significance**: Clara unarmed for the first time. Samuel sees her artistic depths. Lily's first appearance. Courtship crosses a threshold (hand-take).

### Morning After Chapter 4 — Chapter 5
- **Event**: Clara rearmed (leather coat, holsters, Colts, sword cane). Debriefs with Thomas. Thomas: "I haven't heard you sing like that in a while." Thomas delivers the ten-item strategic plan (anti-trafficking). Wallace's art-world patronage and respectability network revealed. Clara orders Wallace's network fully mapped.
- **Location**: Thomas's office, Clara's townhouse
- **Characters involved**: Clara, Thomas, George
- **POV**: Clara
- **Significance**: Wallace thread deepens — power built on social respectability. Thomas dual-frequency seeds intensify. Clara's rearming after the Golden Compass continues the disarmament pattern.

### Shortly After Chapter 5 — Chapter 6
- **Event**: Clara visits Samuel at his warehouse on Front Street. Delivers Thomas's Wallace report. Samuel agrees to help dismantle Wallace's network. Riverfront walk — Samuel shares his father's story (Irish dock worker); Clara partially discloses her father wound ("A Chen does not fail"). Clara returns home carrying something she will not share with Thomas.
- **Location**: Samuel's warehouse (Front Street), Delaware riverfront
- **Characters involved**: Clara, Samuel, George, Thomas (off-page — prepared the report)
- **POV**: Clara
- **Significance**: Clara gives Samuel something she has never given Thomas — a glimpse of the wound beneath the empire. The emotional migration from Thomas to Samuel begins.

### Shortly After Chapter 6 — Chapter 7
- **Event**: Samuel arrives at Clara's townhouse for first training session. Thomas and Samuel meet for the first time — warm handshake in Thomas's office. Clara teaches Samuel martial arts in the courtyard (Japanese yielding principle). Samuel put on the ground three times in three minutes. Thomas brings water and Wallace auction house intelligence (Chestnut Street, 14 lots in 6 months). Wooden practice blades for blade work.
- **Location**: Clara's townhouse, Thomas's office, courtyard
- **Characters involved**: Samuel, Clara, Thomas, George
- **POV**: Samuel
- **Significance**: Thomas-Samuel first meeting. Samuel observes Clara-Thomas dynamic from outside. Clara most physically unguarded (practice clothes). Thomas dual-frequency seeds accumulate.
