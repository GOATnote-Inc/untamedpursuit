## Era 1: Clara's Rise — Fortunes Tide (~1846–1860s)

### Philadelphia Period (~1846–1850s)

#### ~1846 — Clara Establishes Her Empire
- Clara Chen arrives in Philadelphia, builds shipping empire by age 25
- Clara owns private docks and commercial operations
- Clara's townhouse: Thomas Arlington occupies ground-floor office (Persian rugs, tactical maps)
- The Golden Compass music hall established (proxy ownership) — Rembrandt, possible Michelangelo on walls
- **Characters:** Clara, Thomas Arlington, Harper
- **Source:** Episode I 1.31, Fix_beginning

#### ~1846 — The Night Watch (Episode I)
- Clara walks Philadelphia docks at night with George (massive black dog — Newfoundland cross)
- Child's scream — Mercy Jane crew trafficking children
- Samuel Taylor confronting six traffickers; gets beaten
- Clara intervenes with sword cane and multiple Colts — kills 12+ men
- George assists in rescue of two girls
- Clara invites Samuel to tea at 3 PM the following day
- Thomas Arlington given two tasks: anti-trafficking plan + background check on Samuel
- **Source:** Episode I 1.31 (batch6 and batch7 versions)

#### ~1846 — Clara and Samuel's Courtship
- Samuel has tea with Clara; his "jaw dropped when she revealed a fraction of her holdings"
- Thomas Arlington and Clara go shooting, discuss Samuel's character
- Thomas Arlington confirms: Sam is ethical, principled, curious, trains nightly
- Golden Compass performance: Clara plays cello, duets with Lily on violin, sings Italian arias, plays piano
- Samuel weeps during performance; George comforts him
- Samuel and Clara "cross an important threshold" — mutual recognition of depths
- **Source:** Fix_beginning, Golden_Compass_Stage_Episode

#### ~1846–1848 — Clara and Lily's Kidnapping
- Lily Montgomery: daughter of Clara's first business mentor, left penniless after father's death
- Clara gives Lily a position; they are kidnapped from the docks (Henry Drake)
- Six days of captivity on a ship — Clara starves herself, Lily protects her
- Lily passes Clara a metal shard; Clara escapes and kills twelve crew members
- Harper's team rescues them from a small island two days later
- Lily's sanctuary established at Wissahickon Creek: white brick house, heritage apple orchard (Rhode Island Greenings, Northern Spys, Baldwins), terraced gardens, stone gatehouse
- Ajax (dog) chooses Lily; Lily requests weapons and dog training
- **Source:** Lilys_orchard

#### October ~1846–1848 — Kate and Clara in Anatomy Lab
- Clara and Kate Blackwood are medical students
- Clara arrives past midnight, trembling — Professor Matthews says she may not be suited for medical school
- Kate deduces Clara's entire secret life from observation (silk trading houses, dock tar, accents)
- Kate offers nightly lab sessions: "Come or not, I will be here"
- Clara's next exam: second highest score (behind Kate's)
- **Source:** kate_and_clara_in_anatomy_lab
- **Note:** CANONICAL first meeting between Kate and Clara. The 1854 fever outbreak version (below) is harvested for elements but is not a standalone timeline event. The 1875 Colorado ambush is a later encounter, not a first meeting.

#### ~1848–1850s — Clara and Samuel's Marriage
- Clara and Samuel build their alliance into partnership, then romance
- Samuel's financial philosophy: amass wealth, empower employees
- They marry (___Outline NOVEL___ Ch11: "Lessons in Steel")
- **Source:** ___Outline NOVEL___, outline_2_16

#### 1854 — Silver Creek Fever Outbreak (Harvested Elements)
- Fever outbreak in Silver Creek; Kate is the only doctor within 50 miles treating both white and Chinese patients
- Clara arrives with quinine and supplies from family trading networks
- Racist patient attacks clinic; Clara and Kate fight them off together (sword + pressure points)
- They treat the attackers — fever victims regardless of prejudice
- Partnership forms: "Partners then"
- **Note:** NOT a standalone timeline event. Elements harvested for use elsewhere. Kate and Clara's canonical first meeting is the anatomy lab (~early 1850s).

## Era 2: Building the Network (~1860s–1870s)

### Glass Garden Period

- Glass Garden estate built on Monterey coast: redwood and glass growing from the cliffside
- Features: gun ports as architectural flourishes, sword training courtyard, herb garden, hidden passages, conservatory/glass garden, western stone porch overlooking Monterey Bay, study (leather, salt air, aged wine), private cove, armored panels, surveillance systems
- Jack and Kate marry; Eve born (approximately late 1850s–early 1860s)
- **Source:** Claras_Fortress, Part_One_Summer_Memories, Epilogue

### Eve's Summers at Clara's (~1863–1876)

- Eve's first summer at age 5: Clara teaches her the sword — "A sword is not a weapon"
- First lesson: grip, balance, breathing. "Too tight. You're fighting the sword instead of working with it."
- By age 12: horseback shooting, confined-space fighting, advanced herbalism
- Training with masters from multiple traditions: the Chen family martial art (Southern Chinese with Japanese influences), Chinese medicine, European fencing, American frontier tactics
- Kate teaches medicine/anatomy; Jack teaches Winchester and tracking ("reading air")
- Eve also learns Latin, sign language, piano, languages
- **Source:** Claras_Fortress, Part_One_Summer_Memories, Clara_teaching_awareness_16yr_Eve

### ~1870s — Jack's Missions
- Jack completes 14 specialized missions for Samuel over 7 years — "cross-border situations"
- Morgan appears during a fever outbreak 6 years before the reveal — "protecting your family's interests"
- **Source:** Part_One_Summer_Memories

### 1875 — Colorado Ambush
- Kate and Jack in Colorado Territory; snowstorm
- Clara and her brother James Chen ambushed by 8–10 bandits
- Kate operates on James in mining shack; Clara assists with perfect surgical skill
- Jack fights off bandits with Winchester; Clara identifies mining charges
- Chinese cavalry arrives
- Clara proposes partnership; Kate invites Clara to meet Eve
- **Note:** This is a later encounter between Kate and Clara, not their first meeting. Their first meeting was in the anatomy lab (~early 1850s).
- **Source:** Clara_Meets_Kate (Chapter One)

## Era 3: FIERCE — Eve's Story (~1875–1880s)

### Clara's Diagnosis (~1875)

- Eve examines Clara in conservatory; discovers breast mass (left breast) with metastasis
- Hard, fixed, dimpled skin, swollen lymph nodes, blood in cough, decreased breath sounds, back pain
- Clara has been hiding symptoms — weight loss disguised by seamstress adjusting gowns
- Prognosis: six months
- Clara: "Then we'll make them good months"
- Clara asks Eve to promise to help Samuel cope
- **Source:** unravelled_clara, clara_and_eve_discuss_breasts

### The Reveal / Team Formation

- Eve and Jessie ride coast road to Monterey
- Clara greets them at the Anderson estate — visibly aged/ill
- In Clara's study, Samuel reveals Jack's missions and the covert protection network
- Eve: "When do we start?" / Clara: "My dear, you started years ago."
- Team assembled at dinner: Eve, Jessie, Colt, Morgan, Maria, Silas, Dink, Pete ("Crazy Pete")
- Clara has 6 months to guide decisions
- Jessie: "I'm in. Been looking for something bigger than bounty hunting."
- **Source:** Part_One_Summer_Memories, Unit_creation_Shifting_Light

### Clara's Farewell

#### Celebration of Life
- Eve and Jessie arrive at Clara's Monterey estate
- Jessie: uncharacteristically quiet and solemn
- Clara greets them — diminished frame but warm and present
- Clara: "It's a rare thing, you know, to have the time to say goodbye."
- **Source:** Chapter_One_Eve_and_Clara

#### Death Gathering Speech
- Clara addresses her inner circle on the western stone porch overlooking Monterey Bay
- "My breasts seem to be killing me" — dark humor
- "I am a spy. I am an aunt. I am a friend. I am grateful."
- Reveals her blended heritage (Buddhist father, Christian mother)
- Strategically seats strangers next to each other to forge new alliances
- Sign language to Eve: "Your turn now. Make it better." / Eve: "I will. We will."
- **Source:** Clara_death_speech_The_Last_Gathering, Claras_Words_at_Sunset, Claras_Death_Party

#### Clara's Deathbed
- Clara's condition worsens over a week; breathing rapid and shallow by Sunday
- Eve returns from her first mission; delivers river speech
- Shared Latin motto: "Homo sum, humani nihil a me alienum puto"
- Clara: "Then I did what I was meant to do."
- Clara dies peacefully — "passing the torch to the next generation"
- **Source:** Clara_with_Eve_on_her_death_bed

#### Epilogue: Clara's Letter
- Eve discovers leather portfolio under Clara's desk in the glass garden
- Undelivered speech in Clara's elegant handwriting
- Group gathers — Samuel, Kate, Jack, Maria, Lily
- Eve reads Clara's letter aloud: "Be fierce, dear ones"
- Samuel toasts: "To Clara." Eve plays Bach on piano as midnight approaches.
- **Source:** Epilogue_Claras_Final_Words

### FIERCE Era — 4-Act Structure (outline_for_fierce_11_27)
- **Act I — The Hunter:** Eve on mountain ridge, takes "impossible shot" at human trafficker. Returns to Glass Garden. Sees Clara's illness. Thomas Mitchell arrives (separate person from Thomas Arlington in Clara's era; possibly Arlington's son). First chemistry. First attack on estate.
