## Warnings (review needed)

- **[Timeline / approximation]**: Clara thinks "whose first name she had learned twelve hours ago" (line 193). The first-name exchange occurred in Ch-10 around 9 PM (grandfather clock struck nine). Ch-11's grandfather clock strikes seven AM. That is approximately 10 hours, not 12. The continuity notes already flagged this and accepted it as "Clara's approximate reckoning." This is defensible but could be tightened to "ten hours ago" or "last night" if precision is preferred.
  - **Draft says**: "twelve hours ago"
  - **Timeline math says**: approximately 10 hours
  - **Location**: line 193

- **[POV / cross-chapter knowledge]**: Clara references Samuel arriving "in his dark blue wool" (line 195). The dark blue wool coat is described in Ch-10 from Samuel's POV (line 27). Clara was present for Samuel's arrival at her own home and would have seen the coat, so this is plausible. However, the specific detail "dark blue wool" was only narrated from Samuel's interiority (he chose it deliberately). Clara could identify the coat's color and material by sight. This passes but is worth noting as a cross-POV detail.
  - **Location**: line 195

- **[Operational detail]**: Clara thinks about having "tested the seals against saltwater intrusion during a storm off the Virginia capes" and having "sat in the forward compartment once, in darkness" (lines 115-116). These are backstory claims with no prior on-page establishment. They are plausible given Clara's established character (meticulous, hands-on, designs her own operational apparatus) and do not contradict anything in the bible. They read as organic backstory revelation rather than unattributed knowledge.
  - **Location**: lines 115-116

- **[New entity]**: The Quaker abolition circles in New York (line 77) are mentioned for the first time. Clara's Bible profile does not mention Quaker connections. This is plausible given the historical period (Philadelphia Quakers were prominent in abolition networks) and Clara's established anti-trafficking work, but it introduces a new organizational connection.
  - **Location**: line 77
