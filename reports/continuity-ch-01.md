### Harper hearing the fight

- **Issue**: Harper "must have heard the fight and doubled back." But modified percussion caps have ~40 yard effective concealment range (line 86). Harper would need to be within 40 yards or have heard other sounds (child's screams, physical combat, the initial scream that drew Clara).
- **Recommendation**: Plausible as written — the child's screams and combat noise carry farther than the suppressed gunfire. No fix needed unless author wants to tighten.

### Gaslight on the docks

- Philadelphia had gas streetlights by the 1830s–40s, but gas lighting was concentrated on main streets, not necessarily all dock areas. Minor period-accuracy question.
